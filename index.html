My_Philosophy

Open Source

Open source is the engine of technology development; thanks to the giant community of developers around the world. Inspired by this philosophy, I intend to share all my projects and ideas to the public. 

Simplicity

The first character of my projects is their simplicity and minimalism which makes them practical to use in your daily life. This character is also a strong point in order to avoid errors in any program or to maintain them easily.

Efficiency

New technology aims to make tasks easier to perform like mass calculations; that's why my creations respect the equation of time, cost and performance.
